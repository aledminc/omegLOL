WHAT omegLOL IS

A comedy duel with a scoreboard.

omegLOL is a competitive "try not to laugh" webcam game. You're matched live with a stranger, cam to cam. One of you performs; the other tries to keep a straight face. Your webcam reads the smiles and laughs and keeps score — then you swap, tally it up, and climb the ranked ladder. Play 1-v-1, or squad up for 2-v-2.

It's the old idea of talking to strangers online, but with a point: a game, a score, and a reason to come back and do it funnier. Detection runs on your own device and video goes straight between players, so the game is built to be as private as it is silly.
