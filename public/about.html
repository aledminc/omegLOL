WHAT omegLOL IS

A comedy duel with a scoreboard.

omegLOL is a competitive "try not to laugh" webcam game. You're matched live with a stranger, one of you performs and the other tries to keep a straight face. Your webcam reads the smiles and laughs and keeps score, then you swap, tally it up, and climb the ranked ladder. Play 1v1, or invite a friend for 2v2.

It's the old idea of talking to strangers online, but with a point: a game, a score, and a reason to come back and do it funnier. Detection runs on your own device and video goes straight between players, our site only adds to the game aspect.

HOW IT WORKS

Thirty seconds. Comedic Competition.

  1. 01

    Get matched

    Auto-paired with a random opponent, cam to cam.

  2. 02

    Perform

    30 seconds to make them lose it. Bit, face, impression... whatever works.

  3. 03

    Hold the line

    Then you swap. Now they try to break you.

  4. 04

    The camera scores it

    Every smile and laugh is detected in real time and tallied.

  5. 05

    Climb

    Win to gain Elo, rank up, come back funnier.
