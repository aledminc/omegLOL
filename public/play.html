How to play

  1. Get matched. You're paired live with another player, cam to cam.
  2. Know your role. During the countdown you're told your role: the joke teller performs, the reactor tries to keep a straight face.
  3. Perform. The joke teller has the round to make the reactor crack — bits, faces, impressions, whatever works.
  4. The camera keeps score. Smiles and laughs are detected on the reactor's own device and tallied in real time. Bigger reactions score more.
  5. Swap. Roles flip and the other side gets their shot.
  6. Win and climb. Highest total wins the match, your Elo moves, and you climb the ranked ladder. Play solo 1v1 or invite a friend for duos.
