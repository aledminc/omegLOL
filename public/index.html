omegLOL

Talk to strangers. Try not to laugh. It's Omegle turned into a 1-v-1 comedy duel — you get 30 seconds to make a stranger crack, they get 30 to break you, and the camera keeps score.

How it works

  1. You're matched live with a random opponent.
  2. One of you performs; the other tries to keep a straight face.
  3. Every smile and laugh is scored by your webcam.
  4. Swap, tally, and climb the ranked ladder.
