FAQ

Stuff people ask.

Do I need an account?

No — play as a guest. Accounts (coming) save your rank across devices.

Is my video recorded?

No. Video is peer-to-peer and laugh detection happens on your own device; our servers never see your camera.

How does scoring work?

Your opponent's webcam detects their smiles and laughs locally and scores you. The more you make them lose it, the more points you get.

Yes — add friends and queue duos.
